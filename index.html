My Mission

My work is dedicated to transforming education from passive listening into active creation. I design dynamic learning journeys where youth explore STEAM concepts, harness the power of AI to deepen their knowledge, and connect their passions to real-world challenges through innovation and entrepreneurship. My goal is simple: to cultivate a generation of informed, purposeful, and impactful global citizens.

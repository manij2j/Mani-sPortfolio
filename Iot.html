Smart Sustainability: IoT-Powered Innovations

Bridging Climate Science, Technology, and Entrepreneurship to empower the next generation of changemakers.

Duration

12 Weeks

Structure

Pathway-Based

Final Outcome

Group Design Project

Your Role: Become a Changemaker

As a student in this program, you’re not just learning—you’re actively building a more sustainable future. Your tasks include exploring content through discussions and projects, collaborating with peers to build a community of innovation, and applying your creativity and knowledge to address real-world sustainability challenges.

The Innovation Pathways

Pathway I: Environmental Science & Artistic Solutions

Dive into the science of our planet and the challenges of climate change and biodiversity. Conduct field trips, experiments, and research to understand the natural environment.

Pathway II: Technology & Engineering

Tackle coding, IoT, robotics, and more to create tech-driven solutions. Explore the impact of technology towards a sustainable future through hands-on activities.

Pathway III: Leadership & Organizational Skills

Explore sustainable business practices and entrepreneurial skills. Foster creativity as an Eco-Innovator through interactive games, marketing challenges, and real-world case studies.

Guiding Principles

Be Curious

Ask questions and seek new ideas.

Be Collaborative

Work with others and share your insights.

Be Creative

Think outside the box and innovate.

Be Committed

Dedicate yourself to the journey.
